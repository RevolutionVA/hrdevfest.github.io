FAQ

Here are a few frequently asked questions. If you have a question not covered here, email us at info@hrdevfest.org.

  • Why should i attend this event?

    If you are a software developer (or aspiring one) it is a great way to learn about to technologies and gain new skills. It is also a great way to meet and network with others in the community.

  • How can i register for this event?

    Look at the Register section directly to the right of this FAQ. Enter the type and number of tickets you want. Then click Continue. Easy as pie!

  • I am a student, can i attend?

    Absolutely. Contact us at info@hrdevfest.org with proof of your student status and we will send you a discount code for the tickets.

  • Is there a conference code of conduct?

    Yes. Our code of conduct can be found here.

  • Can I submit a talk?

    Please do! Be sure to submit your talk as soon as possible here. If you require T&E and/or a fee please email us at speakers@hrdevfest.org.

  • Can I submit lightning/ignite talks?

    Yes -- we will adjust our schedule for these talks. Please be sure to submit your talk here and note on it that it is a lightning/ignite talk.

  • Can I volunteer?

    Absolutely! We need volunteers! Please sign up here and we will contact you about helping with this event.
